# DOE and power

Define experimental units and responses before factors. Use factorial or fractional-factorial designs to screen interactions, response-surface designs for local optimization, mixture designs for formulations and sequential Bayesian optimization or active-learning loops for expensive experiment–computation campaigns.

Power analysis requires a target estimand, minimally meaningful effect, variance assumption, alpha, desired power and planned analysis. Simulation-based power is preferred for hierarchical, nonlinear or censored designs.
