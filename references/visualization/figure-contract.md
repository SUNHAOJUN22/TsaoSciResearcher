# Scientific figure contract

A figure is an evidence argument, not decoration. Before drawing, define:

- one core scientific conclusion;
- audience and target venue;
- each panel's evidence responsibility;
- source and transformation of every dataset;
- axes, units and scale transformations;
- uncertainty representation and sample size;
- statistical comparison and multiplicity rule;
- legend and annotation semantics;
- export formats, physical dimensions and DPI;
- accessibility and color-vision checks;
- likely reviewer objections.

Quantitative panels must not omit units without a documented dimensionless definition. Axis truncation, smoothing, normalization and excluded points must be disclosed. Mechanism diagrams must distinguish observed links from hypothesized links.
