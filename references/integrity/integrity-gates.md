# Research integrity gates

Audit citations for existence, relevance and claim support. Audit data for impossible values, duplicated patterns, unexplained exclusions and inconsistent sample counts. Audit figures for duplicated regions, inappropriate splicing, misleading axes and undisclosed processing. Audit statistics for multiplicity, selective outcomes, p-hacking and post-hoc hypotheses.

Automated anomalies are screening signals, not findings of misconduct. Preserve evidence, uncertainty and alternative benign explanations. Escalate high-severity concerns to qualified human reviewers.
