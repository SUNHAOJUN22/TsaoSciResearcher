# Scientific writing

Build the manuscript from a question, method fingerprint, results inventory and claim-evidence map. The introduction establishes context, unresolved gap, precise question and contribution. Methods must be sufficient to reproduce the study. Results report findings without speculative explanation. Discussion interprets mechanisms, alternatives, limitations and transferability.

Do not promote association to causation or model output to experimental fact. Calibrate language to evidence strength. Each numerical statement should preserve condition, unit, sample scope and uncertainty.
