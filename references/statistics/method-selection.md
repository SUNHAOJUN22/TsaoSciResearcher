# Statistical method selection

Start from the data-generating process. Identify outcome type, predictor type, experimental unit, repeated measures, clustering, censoring, missingness and sampling design. Check whether observations are independent and whether the model's distributional assumptions are plausible.

Report estimates, uncertainty intervals, effect sizes and sample sizes. Use p-values as one component, not a truth label. Adjust multiplicity for families of confirmatory tests. Separate exploratory model selection from confirmatory inference. For machine learning, prevent leakage, use group/time/structure-aware splitting and report applicability domain.
