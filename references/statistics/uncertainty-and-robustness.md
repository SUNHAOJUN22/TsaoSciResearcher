# Uncertainty and robustness

Separate measurement uncertainty, sampling uncertainty, parameter uncertainty, model-form uncertainty and scenario uncertainty. Use sensitivity analysis to identify dominant assumptions. Robustness checks must be pre-specified when possible and must not be selected only because they recover the desired conclusion.

For predictive models, report calibration, discrimination/error, external or nested validation and out-of-domain behavior. For small scientific datasets, prefer simpler models, regularization, Bayesian priors, transfer learning or multi-fidelity methods with explicit uncertainty.
