# Literature search and evidence mapping

Record database, date, query, filters, result count and deduplication rule. Use concept blocks for population/object, intervention/material/method, outcome/property and context. Expand synonyms, abbreviations, chemical names and spelling variants.

Evidence records must distinguish bibliographic metadata from extracted claims. A source is not evidence for a claim until a precise locator and support relationship are recorded. Prefer primary papers for methods and numerical results; use reviews for orientation and citation snowballing.

Conflict synthesis should preserve experimental or computational conditions rather than averaging incompatible findings. Compare material grade, structure, temperature, pressure, composition, measurement method, model assumptions and uncertainty.
