# Systematic review protocol

Pre-register or freeze: question framework, eligibility criteria, databases, complete search strings, date limits, language restrictions, screening stages, duplicate handling, extraction fields, risk-of-bias method and synthesis plan.

Maintain a PRISMA-compatible count trail. Store excluded full texts with one primary reason. Two-reviewer screening or adjudication is preferred for high-impact decisions. Meta-analysis requires sufficiently compatible estimands, populations and designs; otherwise use structured narrative synthesis.
