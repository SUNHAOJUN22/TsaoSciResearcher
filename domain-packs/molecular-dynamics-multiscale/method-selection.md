# Method selection

Choose against target quantity, scale, assumptions, cost and validation route.

- **classical molecular dynamics**
- **coarse-grained simulation**
- **enhanced sampling and free-energy methods**
- **nonequilibrium MD**
- **mesoscale particle methods**
- **parameter transfer to continuum models**

## Selection questions

- Can the observable be measured or benchmarked?
- Are parameters identifiable?
- Is a lower-fidelity model sufficient?
- Which uncertainty source dominates?
- Which decision changes after uncertainty propagation?
