# Method selection

Choose against target quantity, scale, assumptions, cost and validation route.

- **quantum chemistry and microkinetics**
- **statistical thermodynamics and kinetic Monte Carlo**
- **polymerization kinetics and population balances**
- **molecular simulation and coarse graining**
- **rheology, crystallization and morphology characterization**
- **reactor/process modelling and uncertainty propagation**

## Selection questions

- Can the observable be measured or benchmarked?
- Are parameters identifiable?
- Is a lower-fidelity model sufficient?
- Which uncertainty source dominates?
- Which decision changes after uncertainty propagation?
